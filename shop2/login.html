Just for Kicks is the name of the shop created by the students of ITEC-50. It is a shop that sells a variety of branded shoes. Just for Kicks is a kind of site that is similar to an online shop that helps consumers buy shoes easily by just searching, picking, and purchasing the product using that site. It is also an example of E-Commerce that helps students improve their creativity and skills in doing this kind of site.
